Easy 7-Ingredient Chili

By OpenAI

Ingredients

Amount Unit Item
1 lb ground beef
15 oz kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
14.5 oz diced tomatoes (with juice)
2 tablespoon chili powder
1 onion (diced)
1 bell pepper (diced)
1 teaspoon ground cumin

Number of Servings

Instructions

  1. Cook Meat with Vegetables: In a large pot, cook the ground beef, diced onion, and diced bell pepper over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink and the vegetables are softened..
  2. Remaining Ingredients: To the pot, add the kidney beans, diced tomatoes (with their juice), chili powder, ground cumin, and salt to taste. If the chili is too thick, add a little water to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Serve: Enjoy your chili as is, or with toppings like shredded cheese or sour cream.
  4. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Stir occasionally.
